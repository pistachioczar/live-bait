About Us

Welcome to Howard's Lakeside General Store, Springfield's favorite stop for all things fishing! Established right on the shore, we've been serving local anglers and visiting fishers alike for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on providing quality live bait, tackle, and everything you need for a successful day on the water. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a weekend hobbyist, our knowledgeable staff is here to help with tips, gear recommendations, and the best local spots for reeling in a great catch.
